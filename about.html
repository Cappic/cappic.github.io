The Story

Cappic is an Indian company of a developer cum photographer Manas Khurana which was founded in 2014. The developer got an idea of sharing his innovation with the world and there was no better place for doing it other than Internet. So, he set out on this mission with almost no experience, working hard to shoot pictures and share it on their website to the world.

Cappic specializes in capturing innovative pictures, use the current technology to enhance them and make them even more innovative and creative. With over 2,000 pictures in the company's database each has a number of distinct characteristics and innovation.

Cappic excels in capturing professional level high quality photos each having a specialization of its own. All the photos captured by us can be purchased. To purchase any of the picture, the interested person has to contact one of the developers.
